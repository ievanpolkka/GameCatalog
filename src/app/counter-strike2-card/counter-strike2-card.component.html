Counter Strike 2(CS2)

Counter Strike 2 is a shooting game developed by Valve. The battle is going between 2 teams. Terrorists' objective is to plant the bomb and let the timer reach 0 to explode the boxes on one of 2 plants(A or B). Counter-Terrorists need to hold the timer until the round ends or kill the enemy team.
